## General Information
Didactic Templates offer applying specific permissions to objects with one click. 
Didactic Templates are small xml files imported in Administration > Users and Roles > Didactic Templates. 
Once activated the Didactic Templates are offered in specific object types when creating them, in the respective Settings and Permissions tabs. 
Didactic Templates only change permissions, they do not change settings. 

## Integrated Components
The Didactic Templates service employs the following services, please consult the respective privacy.mds
- AccessControl

## Data being stored
The Didactic Templates module itself does not store any personal data.

## Data being presented
The Didactic Templates module itself does not present any personal data.

## Data being deleted
The Didactic Templates itself does not store or delete any personal data.
Didactic Templates are not moved to the trash but immediately purged from the system.

## Data being exported
The Didactic Templates module itself does not export any personal data.
